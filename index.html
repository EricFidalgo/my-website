How does FloridaBiz Scraper handle nested LLCs?
Our advanced algorithm automatically tracks through layers of nested LLCs, uncovering the individuals behind complex business structures. It continues searching until it finds the actual person's name, saving you hours of manual research.

Yes, it's completely legal. FloridaBiz Scraper only accesses and collects publicly available information from the SunBiz website. We've designed our tool to comply with all relevant regulations and SunBiz's terms of service.

Our system pulls data directly from the SunBiz website in real-time, ensuring you always have the most up-to-date information available. The data's freshness is identical to what you'd find by manually searching on SunBiz.
